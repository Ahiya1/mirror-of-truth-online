### 3. Dashboard - Busy Without Purpose
**Current State**:
- Too many sections competing for attention (Reflections, Dreams, Evolution, Visualizations, Plan)
- Empty states with emojis and CTAs everywhere
- "Deep night wisdom, Creator" - sounds mystical but means nothing
- Stats and percentages (100% Used, 0 Limit) - confusing and distracting

- **Restraint**: Say less, mean more
- **Real Guidance**: Actually help users transform, don't just talk about it
- **Trust the User**: They don't need constant excitement, they need depth

## ðŸŽ¨ Design Principles (Restraint First)

### What the Landing Page Actually Needs
**Not**: Stars, nebulae, cosmic imagery, sacred geometry
**Instead**:
- **Clear value proposition**: What is this? What will I get?
- **One strong message**: Not three CTAs competing for attention
- **Proof**: Show an actual reflection (anonymized) so I understand what this is
- **Simplicity**: Clean, focused, trustworthy
- **Invitation**: Not marketing hype, genuine invitation to explore

### Unified Design System (Earned, Not Decorated)
**Colors**:
- Simple, restrained palette
- Dark background for focus, not for mysticism
- One accent color used sparingly
- Remove gradients unless they serve a clear function

**Typography**:
- Clear, readable, respectful
- Not trying to look spiritual - just being clear
- Generous line height for breathing

**Motion**:
- Only if it serves understanding
- No decoration animations
- Transitions for continuity, not spectacle

**Space**:
- Let content breathe
- Resist the urge to fill every pixel
- White space shows confidence
