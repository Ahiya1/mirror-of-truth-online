# The Soul-Sigh Design Philosophy

## What We're Creating

Not a UI. Not an interface. A **presence**.

---

## The Feeling

When someone enters, their soul should **sigh** - that quiet exhale when you see something that makes time slow down.

**Not:** "Wow, cool design"
**But:** *[pause]* "...oh"

---

## The Architecture of Feeling

### DEPTH (You can fall into it)
Three planes of existence:
1. **Far Plane** - Atmospheric purple nebulae, slow drift, distant
2. **Mid Plane** - Main crystal surfaces, gentle float, present
3. **Near Plane** - Specular highlights, subtle parallax, intimate

### BREATH (It's alive)
Everything breathes:
- Purple glows pulse like slow heartbeat (6s cycle)
- Elements float gently up and down (8s cycle)
- Golden light flickers like candlelight (12s cycle)
- Never static, always alive

### LIGHT (It moves with intent)
Light isn't decoration:
- Purple emanates from within (ancient crystal energy)
- Gold seeps through edges (eternal presence)
- White reflects and refracts (mirror truth)
- Caustic patterns dance slowly (living light)

---

## The Sacred Trinity

### PURPLE - Ancient Amethyst Energy
**What it IS:** The glow of 10,000-year-old crystals in a sacred cave
**What it DOES:** Pulses slowly, breathes, emanates from deep within
**What you FEEL:** Ancient, mystical, alive, sacred

**Technical:**
- Deep rich purple (#7c3aed → #5b21b6 → #4c1d95)
- Slow pulse (6s ease-in-out)
- Multiple glow layers (inner → mid → outer)
- Organic breathing animation
- Caustic light patterns

### GOLD - Eternal Warm Presence
**What it IS:** Ambient candlelight that's always been there
**What it DOES:** Seeps through edges, exists quietly, never demands
**What you FEEL:** Warm, comforting, eternal, home

**Technical:**
- Soft amber glow (#fbbf24 at 5-15% opacity)
- Always present, never dominant
- Edge seepage (light through cracks)
- Slow flicker (12s organic)
- Atmospheric warmth

### WHITE - Mirror Truth
**What it IS:** Pure reflective light, razor-sharp clarity
**What it DOES:** Catches light at angles, reveals, reflects
**What you FEEL:** Clear, sharp, truthful, present

**Technical:**
- Pure white highlights (#ffffff 60-90%)
- Specular reflections
- Sharp edges (1-2px)
- Parallax depth shifts
- Refraction effects

---

## Timing is Sacred

**Fast = Jarring**
**Slow = Reverent**

All timing in this system:
- Breath: 6-8s cycles
- Float: 8-12s cycles
- Flicker: 12-16s cycles
- Hover: 0.4-0.6s transitions
- Never rushed, always contemplative

---

## The User Experience

**Entering:**
- Slow fade-in (1.2s)
- Purple awakens (glow strengthens)
- Golden warmth seeps in
- Everything settles into breathing

**Interacting:**
- Hover: Glow intensifies (0.5s)
- Click: Ripple of light outward
- Focus: Golden ring appears
- Always respond, never snap

**Existing:**
- Constant gentle motion
- Slow breath cycle
- Light that dances
- Depth that reveals itself
